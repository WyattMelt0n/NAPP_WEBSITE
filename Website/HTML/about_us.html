Jeff Novick

Jeff Novick is a dietition and nutritionist with a unique view on nutrition. With over 30 years of experience in his field of research, he offers and new, easy to understand perspective on different topics, most notably nutrition.
Currently he is the Vice President of Health Promotion for Executive Health Exams, and offer lectures at the McDougall Program in Santa Rosa, California. Prior to his current work, he was the director for the Meals for Health community project, helping low-income families become empowered to achieve their health goals.

Along with other accolades, Jeff Novick is known for providing a guideline for nutrition; this guideline offers a new look at choosing nutritious food from the vast field of processed food items. options. Looking at the calories of a food item, the guidelines provide calculation methods that can help determine what is considered healthy and what isn't.
